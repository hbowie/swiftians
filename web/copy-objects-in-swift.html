Copy Objects in Swift

Tags: Swift

Type: API

Protocol: NSCopying

Here’s how to copy an object in Swift. 

  1. Have your custom class conform to the NSCopying protocol. 

  2. Implement the ‘copy(with:)’ method in your custom class. 

  3. Call ‘copy()’ on your object.
